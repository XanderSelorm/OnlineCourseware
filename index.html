Introduction

You will be learning, in this course, the ground rules for handling the various compmuter hardware. The immediate benefits from this course will be the development of your skills and knowledge for manipulating(fixing/managing) computer hardware and the Laboratory as a whole. The course covers an introduction to both PC Maintenance and Laboratory Management.
Well, a blend of reading and written assignments, practical works, handouts, and class discussions will help you acquire these skills and knowledge.
I am sure you will love to know what you must do in order to benefit from all these advantages that PCM 101 offers you. You will find this course fun and interesting to study, but you must also actively carry out all the reading, written and practical assignments designed to help you acquire the necessary skills and knowledge. The course will be run on cooperative, project-based style, and your active participation in class discussions will be essential to realize your expectations from this course. I hope you will put in your maximum effort in learning the material in this course, for doing this is a sure way to succeed in the course. Though your grade at the end of the course is secondary to me, you should remember that if you conscientiously work hard, your efforts would be rewarded with a good grade. What matters most are the skills and knowledge that you will gain from the course that will help you solve real-life problems in the Ghanaian educational and social setting.
